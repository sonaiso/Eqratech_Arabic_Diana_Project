# Augmented Verb Forms System - أوزان الفعل المزيد
## نظام أوزان المزيد والمشتقات الاسمية

This document provides a comprehensive framework for Arabic augmented verb forms (أوزان المزيد), their semantic functions, verbal nouns (masdar), and nominal derivatives, integrated with the triliteral masdar semantic classification system.

---

## 2. Detailed Augmented Form Analysis

### 2.1 Pattern: فَعَّلَ (Fa33ala) - Form II

**Masdar Pattern:** تَفْعِيل (Taf'īl)

**Semantic Functions:**
1. **Causative (التعدية):** Making someone/something do the action
2. **Intensive (التكثير):** Intensifying or repeating the action
3. **Declarative (التصيير):** Declaring someone/something to be in a state

**Nominal Derivatives:**
- **Active Participle (اسم الفاعل):** مُفَعِّل (Mufa''il)
- **Passive Participle (اسم المفعول):** مُفَعَّل (Mufa''al)
- **Verbal Noun (المصدر):** تَفْعِيل (Taf'īl)
- **Noun of Place/Time (ظرف):** مُفَعَّل (rare)
- **Noun of Instrument (اسم الآلة):** مِفْعَل / مِفْعَال (contextual)

**Examples with Full Derivatives:**

| Root | Base Meaning | Fa33ala Form | Masdar | Active Participle | Passive Participle | Semantic Shift |
|------|--------------|--------------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| كسر | break | كَسَّرَ | تَكْسِير | مُكَسِّر | مُكَسَّر | Intensive breaking |
| علم | know | عَلَّمَ | تَعْلِيم | مُعَلِّم | مُعَلَّم | Causative: teach |
| كبر | be big | كَبَّرَ | تَكْبِير | مُكَبِّر | مُكَبَّر | Magnification |
| قتل | kill | قَتَّلَ | تَقْتِيل | مُقَتِّل | مُقَتَّل | Massacre/intensive |
| حرك | move | حَرَّكَ | تَحْرِيك | مُحَرِّك | مُحَرَّك | Causative: set in motion |

### 2.2 Pattern: فَاعَلَ (Faa3ala) - Form III

**Masdar Patterns:** مُفَاعَلَة (Mufā'ala) or فِعَال (Fi'āl)

**Semantic Functions:**
1. **Reciprocal (المشاركة):** Mutual action between two parties
2. **Attempt (المحاولة):** Trying to do something
3. **Directed Action:** Action directed at someone

**Nominal Derivatives:**
- **Active Participle:** مُفَاعِل (Mufā'il)
- **Passive Participle:** مُفَاعَل (Mufā'al)
- **Verbal Noun:** مُفَاعَلَة (Mufā'ala) / فِعَال (Fi'āl)

**Examples:**

| Root | Base Meaning | Faa3ala Form | Masdar | Active Participle | Passive Participle | Semantic Function |
|------|--------------|--------------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| قتل | kill | قَاتَلَ | مُقَاتَلَة / قِتَال | مُقَاتِل | مُقَاتَل | Reciprocal combat |
| كتب | write | كَاتَبَ | مُكَاتَبَة | مُكَاتِب | مُكَاتَب | Correspondence |
| حرب | war | حَارَبَ | مُحَارَبَة | مُحَارِب | مُحَارَب | Fighting against |
| جلس | sit | جَالَسَ | مُجَالَسَة | مُجَالِس | مُجَالَس | Sitting with |
| خصم | oppose | خَاصَمَ | مُخَاصَمَة / خِصَام | مُخَاصِم | مُخَاصَم | Quarreling |

### 2.3 Pattern: أَفْعَلَ (Af'ala) - Form IV

**Masdar Pattern:** إِفْعَال (If'āl)

**Semantic Functions:**
1. **Causative (التعدية):** Making intransitive verbs transitive
2. **Entry into State (الدخول في):** Entering a time/place/state
3. **Finding (الوجدان):** Finding something to have a quality

**Nominal Derivatives:**
- **Active Participle:** مُفْعِل (Muf'il)
- **Passive Participle:** مُفْعَل (Muf'al)
- **Verbal Noun:** إِفْعَال (If'āl)

**Examples:**

| Root | Base Meaning | Af3ala Form | Masdar | Active Participle | Passive Participle | Semantic Function |
|------|--------------|-------------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| خرج | go out | أَخْرَجَ | إِخْرَاج | مُخْرِج | مُخْرَج | Causative: make exit |
| جلس | sit | أَجْلَسَ | إِجْلَاس | مُجْلِس | مُجْلَس | Causative: make sit |
| نزل | descend | أَنْزَلَ | إِنْزَال | مُنْزِل | مُنْزَل | Causative: bring down |
| صبح | morning | أَصْبَحَ | إِصْبَاح | مُصْبِح | - | Enter morning |
| حسن | be good | أَحْسَنَ | إِحْسَان | مُحْسِن | مُحْسَن إليه | Do well/kindness |

### 2.4 Pattern: تَفَعَّلَ (Tafa''ala) - Form V

**Masdar Pattern:** تَفَعُّل (Tafa''ul)

**Semantic Functions:**
1. **Reflexive of Form II:** Doing the action to oneself
2. **Gradual Acquisition:** Gradually acquiring a quality
3. **Pretension (التكلّف):** Pretending to have a quality

**Nominal Derivatives:**
- **Active Participle:** مُتَفَعِّل (Mutafa''il)
- **Passive Participle:** مُتَفَعَّل (Mutafa''al) (rare)
- **Verbal Noun:** تَفَعُّل (Tafa''ul)

**Examples:**

| Root | Base Meaning | Tafa33ala Form | Masdar | Active Participle | Semantic Function |
|------|--------------|----------------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| علم | know | تَعَلَّمَ | تَعَلُّم | مُتَعَلِّم | Acquire knowledge |
| كسر | break | تَكَسَّرَ | تَكَسُّر | مُتَكَسِّر | Break gradually |
| حرك | move | تَحَرَّكَ | تَحَرُّك | مُتَحَرِّك | Move oneself |
| كبر | be big | تَكَبَّرَ | تَكَبُّر | مُتَكَبِّر | Act proudly |
| جمل | be beautiful | تَجَمَّلَ | تَجَمُّل | مُتَجَمِّل | Beautify oneself |

## 3. Semantic Domain to Augmented Form Mapping

### 3.1 Physical/Material Domain (المجال الحسي الفيزيائي)

**Base Classification:** MasdarEvent + MasdarPhysical/MasdarEffect

**Examples:** قَتْل، ضَرْب، كَسْر، قَطْع، شَرْب

**Recommended Augmented Forms (Priority Order):**

1. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Intensive/causative
   - Examples: قَتَّلَ (massacre), كَسَّرَ (smash), ضَرَّبَ (train intensively)
   
2. **Af3ala (Form IV)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Causative (make transitive)
   - Examples: أَخْرَجَ (make exit), أَنْزَلَ (bring down)
   
3. **Infa3ala (Form VII)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Passive reflexive
   - Examples: اِنْكَسَرَ (be broken), اِنْقَطَعَ (be cut off)
   
4. **Tafa33ala (Form V)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Reflexive/gradual
   - Examples: تَكَسَّرَ (break gradually)
   
5. **Faa3ala (Form III)** - Priority: LOW
   - Function: Directed action (contextual)
   
6. **Ifta3ala (Form VIII)** - Priority: LOW (case-by-case)

**Prohibited/Rare:** If3alla (Form IX) - not applicable to physical actions

---

### 3.2 Dynamic/Movement Domain (المجال الحركي)

**Base Classification:** MasdarStateMovement + MasdarDynamic

**Examples:** جُلُوس، قُعُود، صُعُود، نُزُول، دُخُول

**Recommended Augmented Forms:**

1. **Af3ala (Form IV)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Causative (make someone move)
   - Examples: أَجْلَسَ (make sit), أَقْعَدَ (make sit down)
   
2. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Intensive movement
   - Examples: قَعَّدَ (establish rules)
   
3. **Tafa33ala (Form V)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Self-movement
   - Examples: تَحَرَّكَ (move oneself), تَقَدَّمَ (advance)
   
4. **Tafaa3ala (Form VI)** - Priority: LOW
   - Function: Mutual movement
   - Examples: تَبَاعَدَ (distance from each other)

**Less Common:** Infa3ala, Istaf3ala (contextual)

---

### 3.3 Cognitive Domain (المجال العقلي المعرفي)

**Base Classification:** MasdarCognitive

**Examples:** عِلْم، فِقْه، حِفْظ، فَهْم

**Recommended Augmented Forms:**

1. **Tafa33ala (Form V)** - Priority: VERY HIGH
   - Function: Acquisition of knowledge
   - Examples: تَعَلَّمَ (learn), تَفَقَّهَ (acquire jurisprudence), تَفَهَّمَ (understand deeply)
   
2. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Causative (teach)
   - Examples: عَلَّمَ (teach), فَهَّمَ (make understand)
   
3. **Istaf3ala (Form X)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Request/consideration
   - Examples: اِسْتَعْلَمَ (inquire), اِسْتَفْهَمَ (seek to understand)
   
4. **Af3ala (Form IV)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Causative
   - Examples: أَعْلَمَ (inform)
   
5. **Faa3ala (Form III)** - Priority: LOW
   - Function: Attempt to teach/learn

**Rare:** Infa3ala (not typically cognitive), If3alla (N/A)

---

### 3.4 Emotional Domain (المجال الشعوري الوجداني)

**Base Classification:** MasdarStateEmotion + MasdarEmotional

**Examples:** فَرَح، حُزْن، غَضَب، خَوْف

**Recommended Augmented Forms:**

1. **Tafa33ala (Form V)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Show/pretend emotion
   - Examples: تَفَرَّحَ (show joy), تَحَزَّنَ (show sadness)
   
2. **Af3ala (Form IV)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Cause emotion
   - Examples: أَفْرَحَ (make happy), أَحْزَنَ (make sad)
   
3. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Intensify emotion
   - Examples: فَرَّحَ (gladden greatly)
   
4. **Infa3ala (Form VII)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Be affected emotionally
   - Examples: اِنْفَعَلَ (be emotionally affected)
   
5. **Istaf3ala (Form X)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Deem/consider (emotional judgment)
   - Examples: اِسْتَبْشَعَ (consider ugly)

---

### 3.5 Social/Interaction Domain (المجال الاجتماعي التفاعلي)

**Base Classification:** MasdarInteraction + MasdarSocial

**Examples:** قِتَال، خِصَام، صِرَاع، جِهَاد

**Recommended Augmented Forms:**

1. **Faa3ala (Form III)** - Priority: VERY HIGH
   - Function: Reciprocal action
   - Examples: قَاتَلَ (fight), خَاصَمَ (quarrel), حَارَبَ (war against)
   
2. **Tafaa3ala (Form VI)** - Priority: VERY HIGH
   - Function: Mutual interaction
   - Examples: تَقَاتَلَ (fight each other), تَخَاصَمَ (quarrel with each other)
   
3. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Intensive social action
   
4. **Ifta3ala (Form VIII)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Self-involvement in social act
   - Examples: اِقْتَتَلَ (fight intensely)

**Less Common:** Af3ala, Tafa33ala, Infa3ala (contextual)

---

### 3.6 Task/Process/Profession Domain (المجال الوظيفي الإنتاجي)

**Base Classification:** MasdarProcess + MasdarProfession + MasdarTask

**Examples:** كِتَابَة، زِرَاعَة، صِنَاعَة، تِجَارَة

**Recommended Augmented Forms:**

1. **Fa33ala (Form II)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Teach the craft/intensify
   - Examples: كَتَّبَ (teach writing), زَرَّعَ (cultivate intensively)
   
2. **Faa3ala (Form III)** - Priority: MEDIUM
   - Function: Practice with someone
   - Examples: كَاتَبَ (correspond)
   
3. **Istaf3ala (Form X)** - Priority: HIGH
   - Function: Request the service
   - Examples: اِسْتَكْتَبَ (request writing), اِسْتَزْرَعَ (request cultivation)
   
4. **Tafa33ala (Form V)** - Priority: LOW
   - Function: Practice for oneself

**Rare:** Infa3ala (tasks don't usually have passive reflexive)

## 5. Pattern Constraint Rules (قواعد التقييد)

### Rule System for Augmented Form Selection

```
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarEvent AND domain = MasdarPhysical THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Fa33ala(1), Af3ala(1), Infa3ala(2), Tafa33ala(2)]
    prohibited_mazid = [If3alla]
    
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarStateMovement AND domain = MasdarDynamic THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Af3ala(1), Fa33ala(2), Tafa33ala(2)]
    
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarCognitive THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Tafa33ala(1), Fa33ala(1), Istaf3ala(1), Af3ala(2)]
    
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarStateEmotion THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Tafa33ala(1), Af3ala(1), Fa33ala(2), Infa3ala(2)]
    
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarInteraction THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Faa3ala(1), Tafaa3ala(1), Ifta3ala(2)]
    prohibited_mazid = [If3alla]
    
IF base_masdar_class = MasdarProcess OR base_masdar_class = MasdarProfession THEN
    allowed_mazid = [Fa33ala(1), Istaf3ala(1), Faa3ala(2)]
```

**Priority Codes:**
- (1) = High priority - natural and common
- (2) = Medium priority - acceptable, contextual
- (3) = Low priority - rare, requires attestation

## 9. Summary

This system provides:

1. **Complete coverage of all 9 augmented verb forms** with their patterns
2. **Semantic function classification** for each form
3. **Complete nominal derivatives** (masdar, active/passive participles)
4. **Domain-based recommendations** linking triliteral semantic classes to suitable augmented forms
5. **Priority ranking system** (high/medium/low) for natural vs. rare forms
6. **Prohibition rules** for semantically incompatible combinations
7. **Database schema** for implementation
8. **Constraint-based selection rules** for automatic form generation

This framework enables automatic prediction of which augmented forms are natural for any triliteral root based on its semantic classification, while maintaining a lexical attestation layer for actual usage verification.
